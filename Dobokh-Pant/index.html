DOBOKH PANT

199144

Price: 140 euro

Select your size:

Sizing chart

Black and simple. Envisioned as a chino pant with a twist. You can steam iron the middle of the leg for a more classical look. With ankle-length, the pant allows you to show how strong your shoe or sock game is, without you needing to roll the trouser leg. Plus, you won’t have oil stains from your everyday bike commute. Ladies can size down and wear the pant with a high waist for a capri pant look. Both can size up for a 90s baggy pant style. The feature of the twill is its durability, which we find is a feature not a “bug”, because over time, the material will age, adapt to your body and the color will age, as time passes, giving you the one-off unique look.
